# Credits

Survival Cubed bundles third-party assets that are not covered by the game's own
licenses. Thank you to their creators.

## Music

Background music is dimension-dependent: each dimension loops one of its own
tracks (chosen at random) at a reduced volume so it sits beneath the game's other
sounds.

| Dimension  | Track     | Author                                                  | Source            | License       |
| ---------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- | ------------- |
| Overworld  | `0.ogg`   | [bart](https://opengameart.org/users/bart)              | OpenGameArt.org   | GPL-3.0       |
| Overworld  | `1.ogg`   | [remaxim](https://opengameart.org/users/remaxim)        | OpenGameArt.org   | GPL-3.0       |
| Underworld | `0.ogg`   | [remaxim](https://opengameart.org/users/remaxim)        | OpenGameArt.org   | CC BY 4.0     |
| Underworld | `1.ogg`   | [remaxim](https://opengameart.org/users/remaxim)        | OpenGameArt.org   | GPL-3.0       |

`overworld/0.ogg`, `overworld/1.ogg`, and `underworld/1.ogg` were released under
multiple licenses, including CC&nbsp;BY-SA&nbsp;3.0 and GPL&nbsp;3.0; we use them under the
**GPL&nbsp;3.0** option, which permits embedding them directly into the game binary.
