# Survival Cubed

**Survival Cubed** is a multiplayer-first 2D voxel survival game written in Rust.
Mine, craft, build, fight, and explore an infinite procedurally generated world —
solo, on your LAN, or over the internet.

Chop trees and quarry stone, work your way up four tiers of tools, smelt iron and
tungsten at a forge, cook meals over a campfire, and tame a puppy to fight at your
side. When the sun goes down, zombies, skeletons, spiders, and slimes come hunting —
so build walls, light a fire, and survive. When you're ready for rarer ore, craft a
**fire key** and drop into the burning **underworld** below.

## At a glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Dimensions** | Overworld (surface) and the Underworld |
| **Biomes** | Plains, Forest, Mountains, Desert, plus underworld zones |
| **World size** | Infinite width, 256 cells tall, 16×16-cell chunks |
| **Tool tiers** | Wood → Stone → Iron → Tungsten |
| **Day length** | 20 minutes (real time) per full day/night cycle |
| **Multiplayer** | QUIC, LAN auto-discovery, password accounts |
| **Networking** | Encrypted (QUIC / TLS 1.3) with per-server certificates |
