# Skeletons

Two undead archers-of-a-sort. The ordinary **skeleton** haunts the overworld
night and keeps its distance, pelting you with bones; the **charred skeleton** is
its underworld cousin — tougher, melee, and trailing fire. Both are skeletons, but
they fight in opposite ways.

## Skeleton

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 24 HP |
| **Size** | 11 × 16 px |
| **Speed** | 22 px/s |
| **Damage** | 5 HP per bone |
| **Found in** | All biomes, at night |
| **Activity** | Night only — burns up at dawn |

A skeleton **keeps its distance** (closing to about 90 px) and throws **[bone
projectiles](#bone)** at you from as far as ~190 px. The bone flies in a straight
line until it hits you or a wall, so **strafe behind cover** or close the distance
fast — once you're on top of it, it's only middling-tough. Like the zombie, it
**burns up at daybreak**. About 3 in 10 of the overworld's night spawns are
skeletons rather than zombies.

A tamed [puppy](pets.md) is a genuine ally against skeletons — it hunts them on
sight.

### Bone

The projectile a skeleton throws: flies at 170 px/s for up to 3 seconds, dealing
5 HP on a direct hit, and disappears on impact or after its short flight.

## Charred Skeleton

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 36 HP |
| **Size** | 11 × 16 px |
| **Speed** | 26 px/s |
| **Damage** | 10 HP per hit (~1 s between hits), plus a fire trail |
| **Found in** | Underworld **ash valleys** |
| **Activity** | All hours (the underworld is eternal night) |

Where the surface skeleton hangs back, the charred skeleton **charges into
melee** — and **leaves a trail of fire** behind it as it comes (each lick of fire
burns for a few seconds, dealing 2 HP twice a second to anything standing in it).
Tougher (36 HP) and harder-hitting (10 HP a blow) than its overworld cousin, and
it never burns away, since the underworld is always dark. It's a common haunt of
the ash valleys, seeding into nearly **half** of their chunks.

!!! danger "Mind the fire trail"
    Don't fight a charred skeleton by chasing it through the fire it lays down —
    the trail does as much harm as its fists. Strike, then step onto clean rock.

### Charred Skeleton

Not every [prisoner](orcs.md) the new king cast down lasted long enough for the
underworld to remake them. The charred skeletons are the ones who **didn't survive
the wait** — the bodies that gave out before the charred dark could mutate them
into [orcs](orcs.md). The fire that would have reforged them only burned them to
bone, and now they walk the ash valleys trailing the flames that finished them:
prisoners caught halfway, become nothing but the burning.
