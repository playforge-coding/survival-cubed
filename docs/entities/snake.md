# Snake

A desert **ambusher**. Rather than charge, the snake closes a little, **coils**,
then **lunges** across a gap with a fast strike — and recoils to do it again.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 14 HP |
| **Size** | 15 × 11 px |
| **Speed** | 30 px/s normally; lunges at 90 px/s |
| **Damage** | 6 HP per strike (~1.6 s between strikes) |
| **Found in** | Desert (surface) |
| **Activity** | Day & night |

When you come within range the snake **winds up for about 0.4 s**, then snaps
forward in a quick lunge that covers roughly its own body-length and a bit more —
the moment its strike lands is the only time it can hurt you. Read the coil and
**step back during the wind-up**; the lunge commits to a direction, so a sidestep
leaves it striking empty air. It's not especially tough, so once you've baited a
miss, close in and strike.

Snakes seed into roughly **3 in 10** desert chunks, sometimes more than one.
