# Cat & Puppy

Wild **cats** and **puppies** roam the forest. Both are a rare find — only about
**1 in 16 forest chunks** holds one, at most a single animal — and both are wild
until you **tame them by feeding them cooked meat** ([Food](../crafting.md#food)).

Once tamed, a pet:

- **Belongs to you** — you can never accidentally attack your own pet.
- **Follows you**, and teleports back if it strays too far (within ~320 px).
- **Sits on click** — click it to toggle sitting; a sitting pet stays put and
  won't wander, hunt, or follow.
- **Respawns at your respawn point** if killed. The only thing that kills a tamed
  pet for good is **fire** — keep them away from the underworld's flames.

See [Pets](../creatures.md#pets) for the full taming rules.

## Cat

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 8 HP |
| **Size** | 15 × 13 px |
| **Speed** | 24 px/s |
| **Damage** | None |
| **Found in** | Forest (surface), rare |
| **Tamed with** | Cooked meat |

A small forest wanderer. The cat is purely a companion — it doesn't fight — but
it's the easiest pet to keep, asking only that you feed it.

## Puppy

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 14 HP |
| **Size** | 18 × 13 px |
| **Speed** | 34 px/s |
| **Damage** | 6 HP per bite (to its prey) |
| **Found in** | Forest (surface), rare |
| **Tamed with** | Cooked meat |

The puppy is a genuine **ally**. Wild or tamed, it hunts nearby
[skeletons](skeletons.md) and [chickens](livestock.md), biting for 6 HP about
once a second. It also **heals 3 HP** whenever it eats a dropped piece of raw
meat — so the chickens it kills feed it. A tamed puppy at your side is real help
against a night skeleton.
