# Ash Twister

A whirling column of ash that haunts the underworld's **ash valleys**. It barely
scratches you on contact — but it **flings you high into the air**, and the fall
back down is what hurts.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 18 HP |
| **Size** | 12 × 16 px |
| **Speed** | 30 px/s |
| **Damage** | 2 HP on contact — but launches you skyward |
| **Found in** | Underworld **ash valleys** |
| **Activity** | All hours |

On contact the twister hurls you upward and tosses you aside. The contact itself
does almost nothing (2 HP) — the **fall damage when you land** is the real blow,
roughly half your health from open ground and more if you come down a pit. It's a
common haunt of the ash valleys, seeding into about **2 in 5** of their chunks.

The twister is **frail** — a couple of solid hits put it down — so the answer is
to kill it from range or back off before it reaches you.

!!! danger "Mind the drop after a hit"
    Kill an ash twister from range, or retreat before it touches you. If you *are*
    thrown, try to **steer your landing onto a ledge** to shorten the drop — the
    fall, not the twister, is what kills.
