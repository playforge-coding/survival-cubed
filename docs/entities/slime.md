# Slime

The mildest of the monsters — a small bouncing blob that haunts the
**mountains**, interspersed with the placid [goats](livestock.md) that graze the
same slopes. It wanders harmlessly by day and turns hostile at night (or in dark
caves).

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 10 HP |
| **Size** | 12 × 12 px |
| **Speed** | 30 px/s |
| **Damage** | 3 HP per hit (~1 s between hits) |
| **Found in** | Mountains |
| **Activity** | Wanders by day, hunts at night |

A slime is slow, fragile, and hits for little — the gentlest introduction to
combat the world offers. It only gives chase once it's dark and you're within
range; in daylight it just bounces around the peaks. A couple of solid hits put
one down.
