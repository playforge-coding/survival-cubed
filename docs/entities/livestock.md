# Chicken & Goat

The world's passive livestock. Neither will ever hurt you; both drop **raw
meat** when slain. Cook it before eating — raw meat makes you sick (see
[Food](../crafting.md#food)).

Surface creatures seed scattered across newly generated chunks: roughly **2 in 5
surface chunks** seed some life, up to three creatures each.

## Chicken

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 8 HP |
| **Size** | 12 × 14 px |
| **Speed** | Wanders at 22 px/s; bolts at 70 px/s when struck |
| **Damage** | None — harmless |
| **Found in** | Plains, forest, desert (surface) |
| **Drops** | 1 raw meat |
| **Activity** | Day & night |

A chicken pecks idly at the ground and **bolts** the instant you strike it,
fleeing at triple its wander speed for about four seconds before settling again.
A lone chicken pecking the dunes is often the only life in the desert.

## Goat

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 16 HP |
| **Size** | 16 × 16 px |
| **Speed** | Grazes at 18 px/s |
| **Damage** | None — harmless |
| **Found in** | Mountains |
| **Drops** | 2 raw meat |
| **Activity** | Day & night |

A calm grazer of the **mountains**, where it shares the slopes with hostile
[slimes](slime.md). Tougher than a chicken and worth twice the meat, but it won't
run from you — it just wanders.

!!! note "You aren't the only hunter"
    The wandering [farmer](farmer.md) preys on chickens and goats too — it culls
    them for their meat, so livestock near a farmer may not last long.
