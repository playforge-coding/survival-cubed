# Bestiary

Every creature in Survival Cubed has its own page here, with a stat block and
notes on how it behaves, where it lives, and how to deal with it. For the
big-picture overview — taming, pets, combat tips — see
[Creatures](../creatures.md).

Everything in the world is an _entity_. Only your own character is driven by your
client; every other entity (animals, monsters, even other players as you see
them) is simulated by the server.

A quick reference to how fast things move: **you walk at 150 px/s**, which is
faster than every monster on foot — you can outrun almost anything in the open.
Projectiles (bones, fireballs) are the exception, flying at 130–170 px/s.
