# Demons

The underworld's ranged threat. An ordinary **demon** keeps its distance and
hurls fireballs; an **enchanted demon** is one an [orc mage](orcs.md#orc-mage) has
empowered — it flies, hits harder, and fires three bolts at once. Enchanted demons
**never spawn on their own**.

## Demon

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 28 HP |
| **Size** | 10 × 15 px |
| **Speed** | 28 px/s |
| **Damage** | 7 HP per fireball (leaves fire where it bursts) |
| **Found in** | Underworld **charred** expanse (rare) |
| **Activity** | All hours |

A demon hangs back (closing to about 100 px) and **hurls [fireballs](#fireball)**
from as far as ~200 px, every couple of seconds. The fireball flies straight until
it hits you or a wall and **bursts into a lick of fire** where it lands — so don't
linger where it bursts. Demons are rarer than [charred skeletons](skeletons.md)
(about **1 in 6** charred-expanse chunks) but far deadlier at range. Strafe behind
cover or rush them down.

### Fireball

The bolt a demon hurls: flies at 150 px/s for up to 3 seconds, dealing 7 HP on a
direct hit and leaving a tongue of fire where it bursts.

## Enchanted Demon

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 40 HP |
| **Size** | 10 × 15 px |
| **Speed** | 34 px/s — **flies** |
| **Damage** | 12 HP per magic fireball (×3 per volley) |
| **Found in** | Underworld charred expanse — only via an orc mage |
| **Activity** | All hours |

A demon an **[orc mage](orcs.md#orc-mage)** has enchanted. The mage heals it to
full and transforms it: now it **flies**, chasing you through the air, and looses
**three purple [magic fireballs](#magic-fireball) at once** — one straight, two
fanned up and down — that reach farther (~260 px) and hit harder than ordinary
ones. A fan you can't simply duck under.

It never spawns by itself: it's created when a mage enchants a nearby demon, or
arrives alongside a freshly spawned mage that "already" empowered one offscreen.

### Magic fireball

The purple bolt an enchanted demon hurls: flies at 170 px/s for up to 4 seconds,
dealing 12 HP on a direct hit. Like the ordinary fireball it bursts into an
**ordinary** lick of fire where it lands.

!!! danger "Kill the mage to stop the air force"
    Enchanted demons exist only because a mage made them. If you see flying purple
    demons, hunt down the **[orc mage](orcs.md#orc-mage)** before it builds a whole
    air force — but watch the demons it's already made, which can dive on you from
    any angle.
