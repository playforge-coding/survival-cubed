# Horse

A tall, peaceable grazer that roams the **plains** — uncommon, found in about
**1 in 16 plains chunks**. Like the cat and puppy it's a pet: immune to your
attacks, never lost (it teleports to you if you stray past ~320 px), and
respawning at your respawn point if it ever dies. Two things set it apart.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 30 HP |
| **Size** | 17 × 14 px |
| **Speed** | Wanders at 26 px/s |
| **Damage** | None |
| **Found in** | Plains (surface), uncommon |
| **Tamed with** | An apple |

## Taming

**Tame it with an apple**, not cooked meat. Hold an [apple](../crafting.md#food)
and left-click a wild horse to befriend it. (Apples are shed now and then by
leaves.)

## Riding

Once tamed, you can **ride it**. Walk up and **right-click** to climb on;
right-click again to hop off. While mounted you **gallop noticeably faster than
you can run**, and you still jump and fall normally — the quickest way to cross
the open plains.

A horse **can't be ridden between dimensions**: stepping through a fire key (or
falling to the underworld) drops you back on foot and leaves the horse behind in
the world you left.

## Horses & knights

A **wild** (untamed) horse is also a [knight's](knight.md) mount: a knight near a
wild horse climbs on and gallops into battle, the horse soaking blows on its
behalf until slain. A knight won't take a horse you've already tamed.
