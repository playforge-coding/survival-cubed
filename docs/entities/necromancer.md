# Necromancer & Skulls

A hooded caster that never closes in — it kites you and lobs **summoner
fireballs**, each of which bursts into a bouncing **skull** that hops after you.
Left alone, a necromancer buries you under a tide of skulls.

## Necromancer

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 26 HP |
| **Size** | 9 × 13 px |
| **Speed** | 22 px/s |
| **Damage** | 4 HP per summoner fireball — but each spawns a skull |
| **Found in** | Overworld **deserts** (night) & underworld **ash valleys** |
| **Activity** | Burns at dawn in the desert; roams the depths around the clock |

A necromancer keeps its distance (closing to about 110 px) and throws **[summoner
fireballs](#summoner-fireball)** from ~200 px away. The bolt itself does only light
damage — the threat is what it leaves behind: **a bouncing [skull](#skull) wherever
it lands**. Rush the necromancer down (it's frail at 26 HP) before the floor fills
with skulls.

It appears in two places. In the **desert** it spawns at night — about **1 in 3**
of the desert's night spawns is a necromancer rather than a zombie or skeleton —
and **burns up at daybreak**. In the underworld's **ash valleys** (about **1 in 5**
chunks) it roams around the clock, since the depths are eternal night.

### Summoner fireball

The bolt a necromancer hurls: flies at 130 px/s for up to 2.5 seconds, dealing
4 HP on a direct hit. Instead of fire, it **bursts into a bouncing skull** wherever
it lands.

## Skull

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 8 HP |
| **Size** | 10 × 11 px |
| **Speed** | 64 px/s |
| **Damage** | 5 HP per gnash (~0.8 s between) |
| **Found in** | Summoned by necromancers |
| **Activity** | Lasts ~14 s; burns at dawn in the overworld |

A bouncing skeleton skull. It **hops toward you** and gnashes on contact, but it's
frail (8 HP — a hit or two) and **short-lived**, giving out on its own after about
14 seconds. The danger is numbers: a necromancer left alone keeps making more.

!!! danger "Necromancers raise a tide of skulls"
    Don't try to clear the skulls — they keep coming and expire on their own.
    **Rush the necromancer** instead. Both the necromancer and its skulls **burn up
    at daybreak** in the desert and overworld — but the underworld is eternal night,
    so in the ash valleys they never burn, and neither do zombies or skeletons that
    wander down.
