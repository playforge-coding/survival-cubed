# Spider

Fast and fragile, the spider keeps to two haunts: the tree-shadowed **forest**
surface and the **caverns** deep underground. It's the one monster that **climbs
walls**, so it can come at you from angles others can't.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 12 HP |
| **Size** | 14 × 10 px |
| **Speed** | 46 px/s (climbs walls at the same speed) |
| **Damage** | 4 HP per hit (~0.9 s between hits) |
| **Found in** | Forests (surface) & caverns |
| **Activity** | Day & night |

A spider is the quickest monster on the ground, but still only a third of your
walking speed — you can outrun it in the open. The danger is underground, where it
**climbs sheer cavern walls** to drop on you in tight spaces with nowhere to back
off. It's frail, though: a few hits and it's gone. Spiders are a common find,
seeding into roughly **1 in 3** eligible forest and deep chunks.
