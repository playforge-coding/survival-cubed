# Dark Knight

A black-armoured warrior that stalks the **overworld night** in any biome — rare,
tough, and the one foe that makes war on [knights](knight.md) as readily as on you.
It fights like a [skeleton](skeletons.md#skeleton), hanging back and hurling axes,
but it is hardier and hits harder — and a slain one spills **tungsten**, the only
way to win the metal without braving the underworld.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 44 HP |
| **Size** | 12 × 13 px |
| **Speed** | 24 px/s |
| **Damage** | 8 HP per axe |
| **Found in** | All biomes, at night (overworld) — rare |
| **Activity** | Night only — burns up at dawn |
| **Drops** | Tungsten ingots, often a tungsten tool or weapon, rarely a suit of tungsten armor |

## Behaviour

The dark knight is a **ranged kiter**. It keeps its distance (closing to about
95 px) and throws **[axes](#axe)** at its target from as far as ~200 px, every
~1.8 seconds. The axe flies in a straight line until it hits its mark or a wall, so
**strafe behind cover** or rush it down — but rushing a 44-HP foe is no small thing,
and it backs away as you close, peppering you the whole way.

It is one of the rarest things the night raises (about **6%** of night spawns, in
any biome) and shares the per-player night-mob cap with zombies, skeletons and
desert necromancers. Like them it **burns up at daybreak**, so if you can't bring it
down, surviving until dawn works too.

!!! danger "It hunts knights, too"
    A dark knight throws axes at your [knights](knight.md) the same way it throws
    them at you — and a lone wild knight caught in the open at night can be cut down
    by one. If you have a knight, let it close the distance and trade blows while you
    flank, but don't expect it to soak axes forever.

### Axe

The projectile a dark knight hurls: flies at 165 px/s for up to 3 seconds, dealing
8 HP on a direct hit to a player or knight, tumbling end over end as it goes. It
leaves nothing behind where it lands.

## Tungsten in the overworld

Killing a dark knight is the **only way to get tungsten without going to the
underworld**. Every kill spills **1–3 tungsten ingots**, and more often than not a
finished **tungsten tool or weapon** as well — a tungsten pickaxe, sword, or axe at
full durability. Rarely, a kill also yields a whole suit of **[tungsten
armor](../crafting.md#armor)**. Hunt them at night for top-tier gear (and the
tungsten ingots a [fire key](../crafting.md#smelting) needs) without ever mining
charred rock.

!!! tip "Worth the risk"
    A dark knight is a hard, rare fight — but the payoff is the best gear in the
    game. Come prepared (a good sword and room to strafe), take the night kill, and
    walk away with tungsten you'd otherwise have had to descend for.

## Lore

Where the [knights](knight.md) abandoned the fallen crown, these did not. When the
**original king** was overthrown, the dark knights **stuck with the new, evil
king** — pledging their blades to the usurper. That is why they make war on their
old brothers-in-arms as readily as on you: to a dark knight, a free knight is a
deserter, and the only loyalty left is to the throne that bought them.
