# Zombie

The classic creature of the dark — slow, tough, and hard-hitting. Zombies belong
to the night, not to any one biome: the server spawns them just off-screen from
players after dark, in **any biome**, up to **five at a time per player**. They
**burn up at daybreak**.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 40 HP |
| **Size** | 14 × 19 px |
| **Speed** | 16 px/s — very slow |
| **Damage** | 7 HP per hit (~1.2 s between hits) |
| **Found in** | All biomes, at night |
| **Activity** | Night only — burns up at dawn |

A zombie is one of the toughest things you'll face early — 40 HP and a heavy 7-HP
blow — but it shuffles along at a crawl. You walk nearly ten times faster, so you
can always outrun it. Because zombies (and [skeletons](skeletons.md)) self-destruct
at dawn, sometimes the smart move is simply to **survive the night behind a wall**
and let the sun do the work.

A new zombie appears 220–360 px from its target — close enough to threaten, far
enough to give you a moment — and the server stops spawning more once five are
already hounding you.

!!! note "The underworld never sleeps"
    Daybreak only comes in the overworld. A zombie that wanders down into the
    **underworld** — which is eternal night — never burns.
