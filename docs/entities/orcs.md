# Orcs

Two very different foes share the orc name and the underworld's **charred**
expanse. The **orc** is a hulking brute with a devastating slam; the **orc mage**
lands no blows of its own and instead empowers the [demons](demons.md) around it.

## Orc

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 50 HP |
| **Size** | 12 × 15 px |
| **Speed** | 12 px/s — very slow |
| **Damage** | 16 HP per slam (telegraphed) |
| **Found in** | Underworld **charred** expanse |
| **Activity** | All hours |

The toughest body in the world at 50 HP, but the slowest thing on legs — it
crawls at 12 px/s, so you can stroll away from it. The danger is its **slam**: once
it plants its feet and rears back, it's winding up a blow that hits for a brutal
**16 HP** — most of your health in one strike. The hit only lands as its fists
crash down (about halfway through the ~1.1 s wind-up), so **step out of reach
during the wind-up**, then close in and strike while it recovers. Orcs are a
fairly common charred-expanse find (about **1 in 4** chunks).

!!! danger "Don't stand under the slam"
    An orc is easy to outrun, but two slams will gut you. Never trade blows toe to
    toe — bait the wind-up, sidestep, and punish the recovery.

## Orc Mage

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 30 HP |
| **Size** | 10 × 13 px |
| **Speed** | 16 px/s |
| **Damage** | None directly — empowers demons instead |
| **Found in** | Underworld charred expanse (rare) |
| **Activity** | All hours |

A robed support caster. It **lands no blows of its own** — it **shies away** from
you (keeping ~90 px back) and instead **enchants nearby [demons](demons.md)**,
healing each to full and turning it into a flying **[enchanted
demon](demons.md#enchanted-demon)**. It reaches demons within ~56 px and can
empower another every few seconds.

Mages are rarer than the demons they shepherd (about **1 in 10** charred-expanse
chunks). A freshly spawned mage often arrives having "already" empowered a demon
offscreen — better than half the time, an enchanted demon spawns alongside it.

!!! danger "Kill the orc mage first"
    A mage never attacks you directly, but left alone it builds an **air force** of
    flying enchanted demons that pelt you with purple fireballs three at a time. A
    mage also **heals a demon to full** as it empowers it, so a worn-down demon
    suddenly comes back stronger. If you see a mage, **run it down** (it's frail at
    30 HP) before it gets to work — but watch the enchanted demons it's already
    made.

## Lore

Orcs were not always monsters. They were **prisoners of the new, evil king** —
thrown down into the underworld and left in the charred dark, where the place
**mutated** them into the hulking, slow-witted brutes they are now. Whatever they
once were, they have **forgotten everything**: their names, their crimes, the
crown that condemned them. Only the shape of a body kept too long beneath the world
remains.

The **orc mage** fell the same way, but came from higher up: it was one of the
**court wizards of the old king**, imprisoned in the underworld alongside the
others. The mutation took its body like all the rest — but a wizard's craft runs
deeper than memory, and it **kept a fragment of its old magic**. That is all that
survived the change: not the man, not the master it served, only the spellwork in
its hands, now turned to enchanting the demons that share its dark.
