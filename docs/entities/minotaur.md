# Minotaur

The underworld's rare horned **miniboss**. A towering brute — drawn as large as the
[demon king](demon-king.md) himself — that **hulks** slowly after you across the
charred floor, then erupts into one of two punishing attacks. Far tougher than
anything else afoot in the depths, a minotaur is a genuine fight, announced by its own
**music** and **health bar**.

## Minotaur

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Health** | 220 HP |
| **Size** | 22 × 30 px |
| **Speed** | 22 px/s hulking · **~168 px/s charging** |
| **Damage** | 18 HP jump slam (area) · 16 HP headbutt charge |
| **Found in** | Underworld **charred** expanse — **very rare** |
| **Activity** | All hours |
| **Drops** | 1 **Minotaur Horn** |

Unlike the [dragon](dragon.md), the minotaur is a **ground** brute. Most of the time it
just **hulks** toward you, slower even than an [orc](orcs.md) — you can stroll out of its
path. The threat is what it does when it stops plodding: it picks, on a short cooldown,
between two telegraphed attacks.

### Jump slam

When you're close, the minotaur **crouches and leaps**, hangs for a beat at the top of
its arc, then **crashes back down**. The landing sends out a **shockwave** that hits
**everyone standing on the ground** within about 56 px for a heavy **18 HP** — being a
step away is no protection.

!!! tip "Jump to survive the slam"
    The slam only catches you if your feet are on the ground when it lands. **Jump** as
    the minotaur comes down and the shockwave passes harmlessly beneath you. Watch its
    leap, time your own hop, and you take no damage at all.

### Headbutt charge

At medium range — and when you're roughly level with it — the minotaur **lowers its head
and barrels in** at nearly **168 px/s**, far faster than you can walk, goring whatever it
reaches for **16 HP**. There's no slow wind-up: the moment it lurches into a sprint, it's
coming.

!!! danger "Don't try to outrun the charge in a straight line"
    You can't out-walk a charging minotaur head-on. **Sidestep** instead — dodge
    perpendicular to its rush and it thunders past, then commit to a wall before it can
    turn. A charge that rams terrain ends on the spot, so let it crash.

A minotaur is among the rarest spawns in the underworld — only about a dozen
charred-expanse chunks in a thousand seed one, roughly as scarce as a dragon, and only
where there's open headroom for its great height and its leap. With 220 HP and two heavy
attacks, treat it as a duel: bait the slam and jump it, sidestep the charge, and punish
the recovery between.

Felling a minotaur wrenches loose a single **Minotaur Horn**. Five of them, alongside a
clutch of [dragon scales](dragon.md) and a little gold and tungsten, craft the
**[Arena Key](../crafting.md)** — so opening the way to the [demon king](demon-king.md)
means hunting down **both** of the underworld's minibosses.

## Music & health bar

A nearby minotaur raises the same **miniboss music** and **health bar** the
[dragon](dragon.md) does, driven purely by proximity: they kick in once it's close
(within ~340 px) and fall away again when you put distance between you, when it is
**slain**, or when you **leave the dimension**.

## Lore

The minotaurs were born of a hunger and a heartbreak.

It began with **beef**. When the demons first found their way up to the green world and
tasted the flesh of its **cows**, they reckoned it the most exquisite thing they had ever
eaten — and so they drove herds of the beasts down into the underworld, to keep the
charred dark stocked with the finest meat of all.

But the cattle served a second purpose. The same herds were used to **feed the
prisoners** — the wretches cast down by the new, mad king, the ones the deep dark would
slowly mutate into [orcs](orcs.md). Penned in the gloom with only the lowing cows for
company, some of those prisoners pined for the families and lovers torn from them above,
until the ache of that loss bent into something stranger. Cut off from every soul they
had loved, a few of them **fell in love with the cows**.

From that union — the longing of a condemned man and the beasts brought down to feed
him — came the **minotaurs**: half the prisoner he had been, half the cattle the demons
prized, and wholly a monster of the depths. They wander the charred expanse now, horned
and enormous, neither the men they descend from nor the cows they were born of, lowing in
the dark for something neither can name.
